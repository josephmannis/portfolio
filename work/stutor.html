stutor

Fall 2019 | JS, React, Elixir

Stutor (aka TUS) is a web application built with React and Elixir. The purpose of is app is to provide a platform for students to either sign up as tutors and market themselves, or seek out tutors for subject they're struggling with. The app allows for students and tutors to schedule sessions with one another, enter those sessions, and work together with an interactive whiteboard and video chat. This app is a collaborative effort. I was responsible for designing the front end, implementing the search page/features, and scheduling sessions. A live instance is coming soon!
