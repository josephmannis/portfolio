orpheus

Fall 2019 | HTML, CSS, JS, Vue

Orpheus is a concept piece created for my 5D Fundamentals class. It is a website for a theoretical startup that sells a monthly service that allows users to digitally revive deceased loved ones by supplying the data from deceasedâ€™s digital footprint. It attempts to address how we, through the consumption of technology, leave a digital footprint that is not only vast but unique to us. This is what makes this data so valuable - to the point that a price may be put on it. Thus begs the question: how much is one worth: and would one pay a price to bring another back, even for a moment?
