workload

Fall 2018 | Java, Android Studio, Adobe XD

Workload is an app built in Android Studio that was made as a UX experiment regarding time management. The premise of the app is to understand at what locations visited during the day one is being productive/doing work, and providing statistics on that data over time. Users can set up ideal balances for their time, using this app to ensure that they are balancing their life as they wish to.
